# Kyodo Landlord Assistant

**Version:** 1.1.0  
**Date:** June 2026  
**© 2026 Kyodo Partners LLC**

A landlord dashboard that runs entirely in your web browser. Paste a tenant's message and it sorts the request, matches it to your warranties, drafts a reply, tracks rent and leases, runs landlord calculators, and exports everything to real Excel — no install, no account, no server.

## Features

- **Tenant request triage** — paste an email or text; it classifies the issue (plumbing, HVAC, electrical, appliance, etc.), sets a priority, and summarizes it.
- **Warranty matching** — checks the request against your warranty records and tells you whether to call the warranty provider first.
- **Drafted reply** — a polite, ready-to-send response you can copy.
- **Rent & lease alerts** — late/unpaid rent and leases ending within 60 days.
- **Five landlord calculators** — repair-vs-replace, vacancy cost, CapEx reserve, monthly cash flow, and warranty value.
- **Real Excel export & import** — your properties, tenants, warranties, requests, and expenses to and from `.xlsx`.
- **100% in your browser** — no account, no server; your data stays on your device.

> Designed to pair with the **Kyodo Rental Property Assistant** — graduate your numbers into the Assistant for full income/expense/tax tracking and a year-end accountant summary.

---

## Quick Start

1. Open `index.html` in Chrome, Safari, Firefox, or Edge — nothing to install.
2. On the **Requests** tab, paste a tenant's message and let the robot analyze it.
3. Add your **Properties** and **Warranties** so matches and calculators use your real numbers.
4. Use **Export to Excel** to back up your data or hand it to your accountant.

> ⚠️ Data is saved in your browser. Use **Export to Excel** regularly as a backup; **Import** restores it on any device.
