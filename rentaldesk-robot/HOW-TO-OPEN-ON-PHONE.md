# How to Open Kyodo Landlord Toolkit on Your Phone

RentalDesk is a small set of files that run in your browser — no app store, no install, no account.

---

## iPhone / iPad (Safari)
**If you received a ZIP:**
1. Tap the ZIP in Mail/Messages → Share → **"Save to Files"** → pick a folder.
2. Open the **Files** app → tap the ZIP to extract → open the folder → tap **index.html** → it opens in Safari.

**Add to Home Screen (optional):** in Safari, tap **Share → "Add to Home Screen"** — it behaves like an app.

---

## Android (Chrome)
1. Download/extract the ZIP using your **Files** app.
2. Open the extracted folder → tap **index.html** → Chrome opens it.

**Add to Home Screen:** three-dot menu → **"Add to Home Screen"**.

---

## Letting tenants report problems
On the **Requests** tab, tap **"Get a link to share with tenants."** Text or email that link to your tenants. When a tenant fills it out, the request is emailed straight to you — already formatted to paste into RentalDesk in one tap.

---

## Important
- **Keep `index.html`, `app.js`, and `styles.css` together** in the same folder — the app needs all three.
- Your data is saved privately in **your own browser**. Use **Export to Excel** regularly as a backup (and **Export for Rental Suite** to move it into the Rental Property Suite).
- **Works offline** once the files are on your phone.
