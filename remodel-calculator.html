Frequently asked questions

How do I estimate a remodel budget?

Add up the cost of each area you're renovating, then include a permit allowance and a contingency (often 10–20%) for surprises. This calculator lets you enter costs per room or as custom line items and totals it, including cost per square foot.

Will a remodel pay for itself?

It depends on how much value or rent it adds versus what it costs. The calculator compares your remodel cost to the added value and rent increase so you can see the payback.

Is the remodel calculator free?

Yes, it's free and runs in your browser with no signup.

How to read it: Value added vs cost above 1.0 means the remodel adds more resale value than it costs (forced equity). Rent-on-cost is your annual rent bump as a % of the spend — north of ~10% is a strong rent-driven remodel. Rent payback is how many years of the added rent it takes to earn the cost back. Bay Area benchmarks (2026): cosmetic ~$75–150/sq ft, mid/layout ~$200–350, gut/structural ~$350–500+; kitchens $25–100K, baths $25–70K, permits on a major job $15–40K.
