Estimate only — not tax advice. This is a planning tool using 2026 federal figures. It does not include state or local income tax, the optional safe-harbor rules, credits (child tax credit, etc.), or every deduction, and tax law changes. Many people avoid penalties by paying the smaller of 90% of this year's tax or 100% of last year's (110% if higher-income) — this tool estimates the current-year amount. Confirm with a tax professional before sending payments.

2026 federal figures: IRS Revenue Procedure 2025-32. Self-employment tax 15.3% (12.4% Social Security to $184,500 + 2.9% Medicare) on 92.35% of net profit; standard deduction $16,100 single / $32,200 joint / $24,150 head of household.
