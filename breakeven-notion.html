Frequently asked questions

Are Notion templates mostly profit?

Largely yes — after the marketplace or checkout fee, a template is near-all-margin since there's no per-unit cost. The real questions are how many sales cover your overhead and when you profit.

How do I calculate break-even for Notion template sellers?

Divide your fixed monthly costs by your profit per sale (your price minus the cost and platform fees of each sale). This calculator does the math for you and shows the month you turn a profit.
