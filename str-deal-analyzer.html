Short-term vs long-term

LineShort-termLong-term

Estimates for planning only — not investment, tax or legal advice. Figures depend on the numbers you enter; verify ADR/occupancy locally.

How to read it: RevPAR (revenue per available night = ADR × occupancy) is the cleanest way to compare STR performance — it blends rate and how full you stay. Booked nights/mo = 30.4 × occupancy; turns/mo = booked nights ÷ average stay (more turns = more cleaning & more work). The big caveat STR calculators hide: after the 3% platform fee, 20–25% management, cleaning, supplies and full utilities, a busy short-term rental often nets close to — or even below — a simple long-term lease, while taking far more work and carrying more vacancy and regulatory risk. Check your city's STR rules (permits, night caps, bans) before counting on this income.
