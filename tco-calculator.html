How to use it

Choose a module on the left, replace the sample numbers with your own, and read the results. Turn on a module's volume discount to apply pricing breaks — the tier is set automatically by the number of units you enter (5,000+ = Enterprise). HR turnover uses employee headcount, not units.

Depreciation & residual

The IT module can estimate resale (residual) value from straight-line depreciation using a useful-life default you can change — or untick it and type your own residual. Either way you control the inputs.

Make it yours

Frequently asked questions

What is total cost of ownership (TCO)?

TCO is the full cost of owning something over its life — not just the purchase price, but ongoing costs like maintenance, energy, support, and replacement. This calculator adds those up across several years so you can compare options fairly.

How is TCO calculated?

Add the upfront cost to the recurring yearly costs across the years you'll own it, then subtract any resale or residual value. The calculator does this for each option side by side.

Is the TCO calculator free?

Yes — it's free in your browser. A 14-calculator downloadable TCO Toolkit is available for deeper analysis.
